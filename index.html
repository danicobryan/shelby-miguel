Favorite Movies

The Help never ceases to bring me to tears. “You is kind, you is smart, you is important.” YOU MAKIN ME CRY OVER HERE VIOLA DAVIS OKAY. Also, Jessica Chastain and Octavia Spencer are queens and I’m absolutely going to work with them one day.

Favorite TV Shows

Game of Thrones, Friday Night Lights, Big Little Lies, Westworld, Queer Eye, Stranger Things, Haunting of Hill House, That’s So Raven, and Spongebob.

Favorite Food

Homegirl can eat. I love going on foodie adventures and trying different cuisines. I eat it all, but GIRL. There’s a restaurant in Chicago called Quartino’s and they have the BEST. TORTELLINI. EVER. Best tortellini, best fondue, best risotto, best sangria… I could go on for years. If you’re in Chicago, get yourself to Quartino’s. Also, the lobster grilled cheese from LA Cafe is heaven in sandwich form.

Happiest Moment of My Life (so far)

Booking my first national tour, American Girl Live, and having the opportunity to bring two badass female characters to life onstage every night!

(Not So) Guilty Pleasure

I am a Disney freak. I had an annual pass to Disneyland in 2015, and I visited the park 35 times that year. I have Mickey’s Soundsational Parade, Paint the Night Parade, Christmas Fantasy Parade, and Fantastic memorized. And I will watch any Disney movie at any time, especially if it’s Mulan and Coco.
