About Me

Favorite Movies

The Help because it never ceases to bring me to tears. Jessica Chastain and Octavia Spencer are goddesses and I am determined to work with them one day. And for a good laugh, I love watching Baby Mama, especially for the line, “Here come yo Baby Mama ridin’ a Suzuki.” Oh, and also the scene when Amy Poehler’s character, Angie, gives birth.

Favorite TV Shows

Game of Thrones, Friday Night Lights, Westworld, The Flash, Big Little Lies, Gilmore Girls, Stranger Things, American Horror Story (Murder House, Asylum, and Roanoke), That’s So Raven, and Spongebob

Favorite Food

If you know me, you know I love going on foodie adventures and trying out different cuisines. You can never go wrong with Italian because of gnocchi and gelato, and you can never go wrong with Mexican because of guacamole. But I eat everything.

Happiest Moment of My Life (so far)

Hands down, getting accepted to my top two choices for college, the University of Southern California and New York University’s Clive Davis Institute of Recorded Music. I chose USC because #TrojanFamily. Fight On!

(Not So) Guilty Pleasure

I am a Disney freak. In 2015, I had an annual pass to Disneyland, and I kid you not, I visited the park at least 35 times that year. I will watch any Disney movie at any time, especially if it’s Mulan, Beauty and the Beast, and Pocahontas. During my last semester at USC, I had the opportunity to see Pixar’s Coco two weeks before it was released in the States. I have seen [almost] every Disney movie on the planet, and Coco is by far my favorite Disney movie.
