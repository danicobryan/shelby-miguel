Oh hi, nice human!

My name is Shelby Miguel, and I’m a 4’11 Filipina badass from Daly City, California. Super long story short, I grew up a theatre kid going to musical theatre camp at Hillbarn Theatre and the American Conservatory Theatre, and when I was a little teeny bopper, I began and ended my professional dance career dancing for the Golden State Warriors Jr. Jam Squad. First paid job! Yay! Did theatre at my high school, St. Ignatius College Prep, and then graduated from the USC School of Dramatic Arts. Moved to New York City, worked at the world famous Ellen’s Stardust Diner, then booked my first national tour playing Nanea Mitchell and Rosie Imelda in American Girl Live. A global pandemic happened, bringing me back to the Bay, and then back to LA, and this is where I’m at right now! That was the rapidfire version. If you want to look at all the projects I’ve booked, all the training I’ve had, and all the mad skills I’ve got, just click on my resume tab. I just finished a successful sold out run playing Joanne in Amber Ruffin’s BIGFOOT! The Musical, which just won Best Musical at the Hollywood Fringe Festival, so that’s the most recent thing. YAY! Welcome to my website. Have a glorious time staring at my face and reading about me and watching my videos!
